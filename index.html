What We Do

Water Security

We are dedicated to ensure that people in all areas of Timor have access to clean water. Our activities in this area include building basic infrastructure for water access and information sessions on ways to cultivate maintain the new water sources. All this so that one day all citizens of Timor-Leste can access to healthy reliable water.

Water Security

With this initiative, our goal is to promote great opportunities for those in need to learn about how they can take part in their communities development. With access to the right resources, people can become empowered by their own abilities and gain the confidence to fulfill their potential.

Learn More

Disaster Risk Reduction

With our organization’s mission always in mind, we strive to find new strategies for assisting those in rural communities who need it most. In our Natural Resource Management program our team goes into these communities to help improve the livelihoods of the people there and to help prepare the people and the community's infrastructure for natural disasters.
