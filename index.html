What We Do

Water Security

We are dedicated to ensure that people in all areas of Timor have access to clean water. Our activities in this area include building basic infrastructure for water access and information sessions on ways to cultivate maintain the new water sources. All this so that one day all citizens of Timor-Leste can access to healthy reliable water.
