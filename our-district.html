Our District

Whether you have a project with us or just want visit Timor and go off the beaten path, Manufahi is an excellent place to visit with many historic, natural and cultural attractions.

History

Manufahi's history has played a major role in the story of Timor as a whole with great leaders such as Dom Boaventura and other integral moments.

Enviroment

Manufahi has many natural attractions and pristine locales such as the third highest mountain in Timor-Leste and a number breathtaking waterfalls.

Culture

The traditions and culture in Manufahi remain as strong as ever. We have our own specific colors when creating traditional Tais and Uma Luliks.
