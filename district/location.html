Location

Manufahi is one of the most scenic and beautiful places in all of Timor with many natural and historical sites to take in.

Mount Kablaki (Foho Kablaki)

The Heart of Manufahi

The mountain of Kablaki stands as the most notable landmark in Manufahi and is quite literally watching over the people of our a great municipality. It stands at about 3000km high and is known for it sared fresh water springs and is a great place to go for a hike.

Portuguese Pousada (Pousada Antigu)

Historical Portuguese Buildings

These buildings have been in Same since the times of Portuguese colonization, when they were used as an administrative post. Now the buildings are used for various functions as banks, schools and our veryown NGO which uses one othe smaller buildings as offices.

Natural Attractions (Fatin Natureza)

Manufahi's Natural Gifts to the World

In Manufahi, you will find serveral places that inspire awe of the natural world, these places include waterfalls, untouched beaches and cascading mountains. Pictured here you can see a pool made from the springs flowing from Mount Kablaki.
