About Us

Our Story

Here at Luta Ba Futuru, we’re committed to investing our expertise and resources in order to further achieve our mission. Luta ba Futuru means Fight for the Future in Tetun, and everyday we fight for a better future of in Manufahi and ultimately Timor-Leste. In November 2008, Luta ba Futuru was founded in a response to the growing social issues in the district of Manufahi and the surrounding districts. Since then we’ve been supporting our community members in a variety of ways including establishing water security, increasing environmental sanitation and growing the capacities of community members. Just imagine what we can achieve together!
